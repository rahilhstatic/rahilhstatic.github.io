About

Hi, I am Rahil. I design products for people.
I search for the inevitable solution to every problem, i.e., the one objective answer that has resulted from all the analysis, feedback and testing.

My recent projects include a B2B SaaS ERP solution serving 1000+ users annually (NextCampus), a multi-sided B2B2C recruitment platform (WorkIn), and mobile OS experiences downloaded 100k+ times (Nothing).

I build and use my own design library (called Annotate) to turn complex system architectures into simple diagrams and idea maps. I believe that if you stare at the problem long enough, the solution is bound to reveal itself.
