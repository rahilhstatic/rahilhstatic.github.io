Issue #01 (Released: 5 Aug 2022)



→ May 23rd - September 7th 2021 ←

During this time Rahil was working as a UI/UX freelancer, providing branding deliverables & designs for local outlets. He was also consulted for the websites of two companies based in Belgium and Singapore respectively.

However, the desire to find a more stable working home inspired him to look for a full-time job and it is widely considered that he got one.

So, this short lived freelancing period came to an end, but it left behind a goldmine of knowledge and confidence that Rahil would use in his future work...
