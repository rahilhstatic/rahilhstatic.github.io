Issue #3

Released: 17 Jun 2025



→ October 2022 - May 2025 ←

Things have been quiet for two and a half years. Rahil was busy working for NextCampus, Develearn’s subsidary.

Over these years, Rahil has grown into his own as an exceptional designer, having designed complex, multi-layered systems including, but not limited to apps that handle every aspect of college admissions, and apps that allow for Indian talents to search and apply for foreign jobs.

Now with an undeniable portfolio, he begins a new chapter. Rahil bids farewell to the company he called home all these years so that others may have the privilege of experiencing his craft.
