WorkIn

A multi-sided talent acquisition platform connecting recruiters, universities, and candidates. Designed to support direct and mediated (university-assisted) job applications.

My Role

After being explained the vision and scope of the product, I independently led the end-to-end product design which includes user research, UX/UI design, and workflow architecture. This was done for all three user types: recruiters, universities, and candidates.

Process & Approach

Direct communication with stakeholders, clients and company's CEO allowed for rapid feasibility testing of initial concepts. Approved screens got developed and real world product testing was conducted. Based on feedback, screens were iterated on quickly and changes were made.

Results

Delivered a fully-integrated product design with seamless cross-user interaction. The design supports flexible recruitment strategies and demonstrates scalable UX for complex B2B2C use cases.
